## Key Takeaways

1. **Home-care pages** are the most aligned — 15 out of 18 old-site home-care URLs match exactly.
2. **Wellness pages** exist on both sites but use completely different URL structures (`/wellness/...` vs top-level `...-care-in-dubai` slugs).
3. **Blog posts** on the old site use top-level slugs; the local project uses `/blog/[id]` with numeric IDs.
4. Several **top-level service landing pages** from the old site (like `/nurse-at-home`, `/expert-home-healthcare`, `/iv-drips-at-home`) have no equivalent — these were likely duplicate/marketing pages pointing to the same services under `/home-care/`.
5. Three home-care slugs (`caregiver`, `skin-brightening`, `wellness-at-home`) exist on the old site but are **missing from `home-care-pages.json`**.
6. Pages like `/refund-cancellation-policy`, `/thank-you`, and `/company-profile` have no equivalent in the local project.
